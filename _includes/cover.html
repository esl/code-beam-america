Where Erlang & Elixir Developers Unite To Share the Secrets to Scale to Billions
NOVEMBER 03-04, 2022

Erlang and Elixir share the same Virtual Machine, the BEAM. This ingenious little bit of technology is perfect for fault-tolerant, resilient applications that scale to billions of users, which is one of the biggest challenges facing developers and companies today. Code BEAM has united Erlang & Elixir developers for [insert number] of years to grow and progress the community in the spirit of Share. Learn. Inspire.

Share
Across technologies, and industries there are a lot of common problems. Code BEAM is an event where leading developers in Erlang, Elixir and other BEAM based technologies like RabbitMQ share their use cases and success stories in areas such as fintech, IoT, Blockchain, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing and Massively Scale Distributed Software, scalability and parallelism to allow us to grow together.

Learn
We pride ourselves on our connection with community and industry leaders. At Code BEAM events you’ll be the first to learn about new frameworks and applications for the BEAM VM that can increase your productivity and help make your life easier. You’ll often hear from members of the core teams of some of the community's most popular frameworks like Elixir, OTP, LiveView and many more.
Inspire
