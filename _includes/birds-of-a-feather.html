Host a Birds of a Feather (BoF) Discussion!



Do you have a topic you’re passionate about or a challenge you’d love to explore with peers? Hosting a BoF session is your chance to bring it to life!

BoF (Birds of a Feather) sessions are informal, attendee-driven discussions where small groups (up to 10 people) gather to dive into shared interests or big questions. No slides, no formalities—just great conversations.


How It Works:
- Share your topic idea via the submission link included in your welcome email.
- Topics with at least 5 votes will get their own table.
- If your topic is selected, you’ll host the table and guide the discussion.

Some Pre-Selected Topics:
- Embedded and Frameworks
- Language/Tool Adoption
- Domain-Driven Design
- Designing for Scalability and Reliability
- Technical Debt

Want to see your topic on this list? Keep an eye on your welcome email for submission details, and rally your peers to vote for your idea!
